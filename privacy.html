Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

At Code Punch Bakery, your digital focus is your own business. We build tools that help you manage your workflow, not your data. This policy explains our commitment to transparency and privacy for the FocusFlow extension.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • Zero Personal Data: FocusFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All your extension settings (tab limits, whitelists, and license keys) are stored locally within your browser or synced via your private Google Chrome Profile. We never have access to this data.
  • No Browsing History Tracking: While the extension monitors tab counts to manage your limits, it does not log, track, or sell your browsing history.

2. Third-Party Services

We partner with Lemon Squeezy to handle secure payment processing. When you purchase a Pro license:

  • Lemon Squeezy collects your payment and billing information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.
  • Code Punch Bakery only receives a confirmation of purchase and your generated license key. We do not see your credit card details.

3. Chrome Permissions

FocusFlow requires specific permissions to function:

  • Tabs: To count and manage your active tabs.
  • Storage: To save your custom preferences and Master Baker activation status.
  • Notifications: To alert you when you've reached your tab limit.

4. Data Security

Since your data lives on your device, it is as secure as your browser. We recommend keeping Chrome updated to the latest version to ensure maximum security for your local data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, feel free to reach out to us at codepunchbakery@gmail.com.
