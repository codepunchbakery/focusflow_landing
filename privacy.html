Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

At Code Punch Bakery, your privacy is a core ingredient. We build tools that help you manage your workflow, not your data. This policy explains our commitment to transparency for the FocusFlow extension.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • Zero Personal Data: FocusFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All settings (tab limits, whitelists, and license keys) are stored locally in your browser. We never have access to this data.
  • No Tracking: We do not log, track, or sell your browsing history.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Gumroad to process secure payments. When you purchase a Master Baker license:

  • Gumroad collects your billing information in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • Code Punch Bakery only receives a confirmation of purchase and your license key. We do not see your credit card details.

3. Chrome Permissions

FocusFlow requires specific permissions to function:

  • Tabs: To count and manage your active tabs.
  • Storage: To save your preferences and activation status.

4. Contact Us

Questions? Reach out to us at codepunchbakery@gmail.com.
